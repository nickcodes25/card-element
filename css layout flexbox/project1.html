Gujarat

Certainly! Gujarat is a state located on the western coast of India. Here are some key facts about Gujarat: Capital and Largest City: The capital of Gujarat is Gandhinagar, while its largest city is Ahmedabad.

Geography: Gujarat is known for its diverse geography, including the Arabian Sea coastline, the Great Rann of Kutch, and various mountain ranges. The state shares its borders with Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, and the union territories of Daman and Diu and Dadra and Nagar Haveli.
